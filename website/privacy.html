Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

The short version: Your snippets stay on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit your text data. Period.

What data we collect

SnapType is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's exactly what we do and don't collect:

We do NOT collect:

  • Your snippet content or shortcut text
  • Text you type on any website
  • Browsing history or website URLs
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Keystroke data or typing patterns
  • Any data from pages you visit

We DO store locally on your device:

  • Your snippets and shortcuts (using Chrome's local storage API)
  • Your settings and preferences
  • Aggregate usage statistics (total expansion count, keystrokes saved)
  • License key status (if you have a Pro subscription)

How your data is stored

All snippet data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to export it.

We do not operate any servers that receive or store your snippet data.

Pro subscription

If you purchase SnapType Pro, payment is processed by Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider. We receive a license key associated with your purchase but do not directly handle your payment information. Please refer to Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

The only data transmitted for Pro users is the license key validation check, which verifies whether your license is active. No snippet data is included in this request.

Snippet Packs

When you import a snippet pack, the extension fetches a JSON file from our GitHub repository. This is a standard HTTP request and does not transmit any of your personal data or snippet content.

Third-party services

SnapType does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs. We do not share data with any third parties.

Permissions explained

  • storage: To save your snippets and settings locally on your device
  • activeTab: To expand snippets in the currently active tab when you use a shortcut
  • contextMenus: To add the "Save as SnapType snippet" option when you right-click selected text

Data deletion

You can delete all your SnapType data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension (removes all local data)
  • Using Chrome's "Clear browsing data" to clear extension storage
  • Deleting individual snippets through the SnapType popup

Children's privacy

SnapType does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@snaptype.app.
