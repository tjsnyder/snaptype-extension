How We Calculate Your Savings

Our ROI calculator uses a straightforward formula based on your actual typing patterns:

  • Total characters per day = messages per day x average characters per message
  • Words per day = total characters / 5 (standard word length)
  • Time spent typing = words per day / your typing speed (WPM)
  • Time saved = typing time x 90% (text expanders eliminate the need to type repetitive content, reducing input to a short trigger)
  • Money saved = time saved x your hourly rate

The 90% time reduction is a conservative estimate. With SnapType, you type a short shortcut (e.g., /thanks) and your full message expands instantly. Instead of typing 150+ characters, you type 7. That is over 95% fewer keystrokes in practice.
