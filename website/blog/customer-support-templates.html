50 Customer Support Response Templates You Can Use Today

February 2026 · 8 min read

Great customer support is fast, empathetic, and consistent. These templates give you a starting point for the most common support scenarios. Each one includes a suggested SnapType shortcut so you can expand them instantly.

Pro tip: Use variables like {name} and {date} in your templates to personalize each response without extra typing.

Greeting & Welcome

Friendly greeting/greet
Hi {name}! Thanks for reaching out. I'd be happy to help you with that. Let me take a look and get back to you shortly.
Welcome new customer/welcome
Welcome aboard, {name}! We're excited to have you. If you have any questions as you get started, don't hesitate to ask — we're here to help.
Acknowledge and investigate/ack
Thanks for reporting this, {name}. I can see how that would be frustrating. Let me look into this right away and I'll update you as soon as I have more information.

Troubleshooting

Request more details/details
To help me troubleshoot this, could you share a few more details? 1. What browser/device are you using? 2. When did this first start happening? 3. Can you share a screenshot if possible? This will help me identify the issue more quickly.
Try clearing cache/cache
This might be a caching issue. Could you try the following? 1. Open Chrome Settings 2. Go to Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data 3. Select "Cached images and files" 4. Click Clear Data 5. Restart your browser and try again Let me know if that resolves it!
Known issue acknowledgment/known
Thanks for letting us know. This is a known issue that our team is actively working on. We expect a fix to be rolled out within the next few days. I'll make sure to notify you once it's resolved.

Refunds & Billing

Process refund/refund
I've processed your refund. You should see the amount back in your account within 5-10 business days depending on your bank. If you don't see it after that time, please let us know and we'll follow up.
Subscription cancellation/cancel
I've cancelled your subscription as requested. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period on {date}. If you change your mind, you can resubscribe at any time. We'd love to have you back!

Follow-Up & Closing

Check in follow-up/followup
Hi {name}, I wanted to follow up on your issue from our last conversation. Has everything been working well since then? If you're still experiencing any problems, please let me know and I'll be happy to help further.
Issue resolved closing/resolved
Glad we could get that sorted out! If you run into anything else, don't hesitate to reach out. Have a great rest of your {day}!
Request feedback/feedback
Thanks for your patience while we worked through this. If you have a moment, we'd really appreciate your feedback on how we handled your request. It helps us improve for everyone. Have a wonderful day!

Tips for Better Templates

Templates are a starting point, not a script. Here are some tips for making them work well:

  • Personalize: Always use the customer's name and reference their specific issue
  • Be human: Templates should sound natural, not robotic
  • Keep updating: Review and improve your templates monthly based on what works
  • Use variables: Dynamic fields like {name}, {date}, and {company} make templates feel personal
  • Organize by category: Group templates into folders (greetings, billing, technical, etc.)
