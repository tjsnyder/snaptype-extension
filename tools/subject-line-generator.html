Pro Tips for Email Subject Lines

  • Keep it under 50 characters for full visibility on mobile devices
  • Use the recipient's name or company for 26% higher open rates
  • Numbers and lists create specificity that drives clicks
  • Create urgency without being spammy (avoid ALL CAPS and excessive punctuation!!!)
  • A/B test your top 2 candidates with a small segment before full send
  • Avoid spam triggers: "free", "act now", "limited time", excessive emojis
  • Preview text (preheader) is your subject line's wingman: use it
  • Question-based subjects get 10% more opens than statements
